Frequently asked

Can I switch plans later?

Yes — you can upgrade or downgrade at any time; the Eternal Plan can be added on top of either subscription tier.

What happens if I miss a heartbeat check-in?

Nothing immediately. A missed check-in only begins the guardian verification sequence after your chosen inactivity window — guardians must still reach consensus before anything is released.

Is my data ever shared with Reep employees?

No. Reep uses zero-knowledge encryption — your vault contents are unreadable to our staff and servers by design.
